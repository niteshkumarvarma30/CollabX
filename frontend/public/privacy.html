Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

1. Introduction

CollabX ("we", "our", or "us") provides an Instagram Insights Dashboard that helps users view analytics for their Instagram Business or Creator accounts. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

When you connect your Instagram account through Facebook Login, we access:

  • Facebook Page information: Page name and ID for pages you manage.
  • Instagram Business/Creator account data: Username, follower count, and account ID.
  • Instagram media insights: Post reach, likes, comments, saved count, shares, and engagement rates.

We do not collect your Facebook or Instagram password. Authentication is handled securely through Meta's OAuth system.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data solely to:

  • Display your Instagram analytics and engagement metrics on your dashboard.
  • Calculate engagement rates and identify top-performing posts.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your access tokens and dashboard data are stored securely in an encrypted database. We do not share, sell, or distribute your data to any third parties.

5. Data Retention

Your session data is retained for as long as you use the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Meta Platform (Facebook/Instagram): For authentication and accessing Instagram insights via the Graph API.
  • Render: For hosting our application.
  • Supabase: For secure database storage.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access to your Facebook/Instagram data at any time through your Facebook Business Integrations settings.
  • Request deletion of your stored data.
